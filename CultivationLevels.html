Cultivation Levels

Cultivation Level Breakdown
Level Name Lifespan Description
1 Apprentice(1-9) 100yrs High level practitioners in low level countries of 'aboriginal' planets.
In this stage, the practitioner strengthens their body and consciousness to the utmost level with genetic energy.
2 Planetary(1-9) 1000yrs Cultivators at this stage form a Miniature Planet inside their body.
This Planet also contains the essence of an Original Law.
This planet has its own gravitational pull which can counteract
the weaker gravitational pulls of planets, allowing flight. Practitioners at this level are barely considered
basic cultivators in the universe and can only be thugs, servants or cannon fodder.
With a punch they can destroy a small town, and fly at supersonic speeds
depending on the strength of the planet's gravity.
3 Stellar(1-9) 1 Era (10k years) The Planetary System within oneself is transformed into a Star System.
They cannot survive in space for a long time, but can fly short distances.
Basically all the features of a planet level upgraded.
One punch can destroy a large city.
In the entire galaxy they are ordinary soldiers,
bodyguards or cannon fodder.
4 Universe(1-9) 10 Eras (100k years) On average 1 Universe Level is born in each Galaxy. A Universe Level 1 is 40 times stronger than an average Stellar Level 9. At this level ones spiritual energy (mind), physical energy (body), genetic energy and will naturally reach an incomparably beautiful and harmonious state and the fusion of these forces creates a miniature black hole, that produces energy that can have a controlling effect on the surrounding space.
5 Domain Lord(1-9) 100 Eras (1 Million years) Once you have reached the Domain Master level you will typically start to step into the threshold of a lower law. A fusion of the 9 black holes will commence to form one big black hole. Energy will be absorbed and solidified to create a life core. This life core stores the soul of that domain lordThe life core can be in any part of their body. Because the life core of a domain lord needs to be destroyed in order to kill them, domain lords are capable of surviving a destroyed head and domain lord-level slaves are rare. (Slaves tend to be controlled via a chip implanted in the brain) A domain lord powerhouse is powerful enough to destroy a small planet with a punch.
6 World Lord(1-9) 1,000 Eras (10 Million years) World Lords try to obtain a deep understanding of a certain lower source of the law, and even get the recognition of the source of the law. They also began to have a preliminary understanding of the superior laws (usually the space law). The life core formed during the domain lord stage transforms into a miniature World. One sector lord tends to appear for every 1000 galaxies. Sector lords are capable of storing and carrying lifeforms around in their miniature world. One punch can easily destoy a small star system. eg:Ouru Solar System As sector lords are capable of maintaining their youth, they will only perish after 1,000 eras(or 10 million years) when their soul collapses.
7 Immortal(Normal-King) 1 Reincarnation Era(Lifespan of the Universe) They comprehend a certain low-level original law (gold, wood, water, fire, earth, wind, thunder, and light), and gradually comprehend the corresponding upper-level original law (usually the space law). Cultivators who through the lower level of the origin laws (generally 90%+ understanding of a law), formed a divine body, and managed to imprint a soul imprint on every part of their body. The mini world inside the body is released into a part of space preferably a hidden area, to form an immortal kingdom. In this kingdom is a vast ammount of energy, which can be accessed by the owner at any time. They Every Immortal/Immortal King has a known title as a moniker. E.g. Blade River King. As every cell of an Immortal contains part of their soul, Immortal Kings can be reborn from a single cell. Attacks from a star railgun that can vaporize galaxies are incapable of harming an Immortal.
8 Universe Venerable 1 Reincarnation Era(Lifespan of the Universe) There are two ways of becoming a Universe Venerable. Comprehending any Lower Law and either comprehending the Law of Space or the Law of Time and all their talents. Imperfectly fusing any Lower and Upper Law. For example the Beast Gods follow this path. Further divided into Space Venerables and Time Venerables. 1000 times stronger than an ordinary Immortal.
9 Universe Master(Tier 1-6) 1 Reincarnation Era(Lifespan of the Universe) There are two ways of becoming a Universe Master. Comprehending both the Law of Space and Law of Time and any Lower Law. Perfectly fusing any Lower and Upper Law. For example the Beast Gods follow this path. When a Universe Venerable breaks through to become the Universe Master then the Original Ocean with a diameter of tens of billions of kilometers descends, transforming their Kingdom of God and increasing their divine power by a hundredfold. As such they have transcended Space & Time and became a Universe Master. Within a certain space around them, they can stop the Original Laws Operation, however as powerful as they are, they still cannot detect their Operation. A singular blow from a Universe Master makes a normal Universe Venerable lose 10% of their Divine Body.
10 Supreme Universe Master/ True god (Tier 7-9) 3 Riencarnation Eras There are two ways of breaking through to True God When someone breaks through, the Original Universe will protect that person and prevent any form of interruption. Their Kingdom of God will be transformed into a Miniature Universe, independent of the original universe they were born in which is why they can live, longer than the universe.
