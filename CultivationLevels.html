Cultivation Levels

Cultivation Level Breakdown
Level Name Lifespan Description
1 Apprentice (1-9) 100 yrs High-level practitioners in low-level countries of 'aboriginal' planets. In this stage, the practitioner strengthens their body and consciousness to the utmost level with genetic energy.
2 Planetary (1-9) 1,000 yrs Cultivators at this stage form a Miniature Planet inside their body, containing the essence of an Original Law. They can fly at supersonic speeds and destroy small towns with a punch.
3 Stellar (1-9) 1 Era (10k yrs) The Planetary System within oneself is transformed into a Star System. They can destroy large cities and serve as ordinary soldiers or bodyguards in the galaxy.
4 Universe (1-9) 10 Eras (100k yrs) At this level, spiritual, physical, and genetic energies fuse to create a miniature black hole, granting control over surrounding space. One is 40x stronger than a Stellar Level 9.
5 Domain Lord (1-9) 100 Eras (1 Million yrs) Domain Lords fuse 9 black holes into one, forming a life core that stores their soul. They can survive severe injuries and destroy small planets with a punch.
6 World Lord (1-9) 1,000 Eras (10 Million yrs) World Lords gain deep understanding of a lower law and form a miniature world inside their body. They can store lifeforms and destroy star systems.
7 Immortal (Normal-King) 1 Reincarnation Era (Universe lifespan) Immortals comprehend original laws, form a divine body, and create an immortal kingdom. They can regenerate from a single cell and are nearly indestructible.
8 Universe Venerable 1 Reincarnation Era (Universe lifespan) Universe Venerables fuse lower and upper laws, becoming 1,000 times stronger than ordinary Immortals. They are divided into Space and Time Venerables.
9 Universe Master (Tier 1-6) 1 Reincarnation Era (Universe lifespan) Masters perfectly fuse laws and transcend space and time, gaining immense power. They can stop the operation of original laws within a certain space.
10 Supreme Universe Master / True God (Tier 7-9) 3 Reincarnation Eras True Gods transform their Kingdom of God into a Miniature Universe, becoming independent of the original universe and living longer than the universe itself.
