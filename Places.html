Locations from Swallowed Star

Cang Lan Planet

A mysterious and resource-rich planet, Cang Lan is known for its lush landscapes and unique wildlife. It serves as a pivotal location for many adventures and discoveries in the Swallowed Star universe.

Bo Mo Planet

Bo Mo Planet is famous for its advanced civilization and technological prowess. It is a hub for interstellar trade and a meeting point for various alien races.

Horned Dragon Planet

This planet is dominated by the powerful Horned Dragon clan. Its harsh environment and formidable inhabitants make it a challenging destination for any traveler.

Luo Family Castle

The ancestral home of the Luo family, this castle stands as a symbol of strength and heritage. It is a place of refuge and strategy throughout the story.

Horned Dragon City

A bustling metropolis on the Horned Dragon Planet, this city is renowned for its towering architecture and vibrant culture, as well as its fierce defenders.
