Characters from Swallowed Star

Luo Feng

The main protagonist, Luo Feng rises from humble beginnings to become one of the most powerful beings in the universe. His determination, intelligence, and compassion drive his journey through countless challenges.

Primal Chaos City Leader

A legendary figure and mentor to many, the Primal Chaos City Leader is renowned for his wisdom and strength. He plays a crucial role in guiding Luo Feng and shaping the fate of countless warriors.

Ai Chen

Ai Chen is a talented member of the dream winged clan. Her courage and resourcefulness make her an genius in the original series.

Brolin

Ambitious and cunning, Brolin is both a rival and a formidable opponent. His quest for power often puts him at odds with Luo Feng and other heroes.

Sitting Mountain Guest

A mysterious and enigmatic figure, Sitting Mountain Guest possesses vast knowledge and unique abilities. His true motives are often hidden, making him an intriguing character in the story.
